I am an MSc student in Experimental Nuclear and Particle Physics at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and the Institute of High Energy Physics (IHEP) in Beijing, China. My academic path includes working as a Physics Laboratory Assistant at Samarkand State University and conducting research internships at international centers like JINR.

### Core Research Areas
* **High Energy Physics Experiments**: Participating in advanced physics analysis and software training sessions, including the CMS Data Analysis School (CMSDAS).
* **Standard Model & Higgs Physics**: Focused on precise measurements of Standard Model physics and exploring Beyond the Standard Model (BSM) physics.
* **Experimental Data Processing**: Experienced in tracking and processing data from photonuclear emulsions, bubble chambers, and multinucleon transfer reactions.

### Technical Expertise
* **Programming & Tools**: Python, LaTeX, and OriginLab.
* **Laboratory Skills**: Experimental data processing, bubble chamber data analysis, and optical microscope operations.

### Academic Background
* **MSc in Experimental Nuclear and Particle Physics** (2025 – Present) – University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) & Institute of High Energy Physics (IHEP), Beijing, China.
* **BSc in Physics** (2020 – 2024) – National University of Uzbekistan, Tashkent, Uzbekistan.
